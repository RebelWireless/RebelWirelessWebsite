Frequently Asked Questions

Learn how to connect to Rebel Wireless using BYOD or Ubiquiti radios.

General

What is a WISP? ▼

A Wireless Internet Service Provider (WISP) delivers internet access over radio links instead of fiber or cable. Small antennas connect wirelessly to our network.

Why choose a WISP? ▼

WISPs offer flexibility in areas without wired infrastructure, faster setup, and community-focused connectivity.

Equipment & Installation

Can I use my own equipment (BYOD)? ▼

Yes. BYOD devices are supported if compatible with our frequencies and network setup.

What equipment do you provide? ▼

We provide Ubiquiti radios such as LiteBeam, NanoBeam, or airMAX devices depending on your location.

Can I install it myself? ▼

Many customers self-install. Installation involves mounting the radio, aiming it, and running Ethernet indoors.

Community Networks (NYC Mesh)

What is NYC Mesh? ▼

NYC Mesh is a community-run wireless network connecting neighborhoods using rooftop radios. It's a good example of decentralized networking.

How is this different from a WISP? ▼

A WISP provides dedicated service and formal support, while mesh networks rely on shared community resources.
