Rebel Wireless is a local Wireless Internet Service Provider (WISP). We deliver high-speed internet using licensed and unlicensed 5GHz Ubiquiti equipment instead of cable or fiber lines.
We install a small outdoor antenna on your home that connects to our nearest tower using secure 5GHz radio frequencies. That antenna connects to a router inside your home, providing WiFi just like cable internet.
We use professional-grade Ubiquiti 5GHz wireless radios and antennas designed specifically for WISP deployments. This equipment is reliable, secure, and optimized for long-distance connections.
